About Drake

I make songs that feel like late‑night drives: hushed, honest, and a little cinematic. I love the drama of glam and the intimacy of folk — somewhere between Bowie’s theater and Fleetwood Mac’s glow. If you’re into pop‑folk leaning melodies with a confessional edge, you’re in the right place.

Based in Texas • Manager/host at Hico Hall • Building the first releases one show at a time.
